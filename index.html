My Journey

Hey there! I'm Yacoub, a tech enthusiast who turned passion into a profession. I graduated with a First Class Honors Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science from the University of Kent, where I dived deep into AI and developed an image enhancement application that's nothing short of magic. But that's not where my story began. My foundation was laid with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, also from Kent, where I brought a campus navigation chatbot to life. It's been an exhilarating ride, and I'm just getting started!

What I've Been Up To

My professional journey has been a roller coaster of learning and growth. As a FullStack Intern at ProgressSoft, I got my hands dirty with full-stack development, making applications more scalable and intuitive. Then, at RealSoft, I worked on a project that developed a Python-based visual recognition system. These experiences taught me not just about coding, but about solving real-world problems with technology.
